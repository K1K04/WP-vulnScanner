# ✨ PDF Enhancement Report

## What Was Improved

### 📄 Technical PDF (pdf_gen.py)

#### Metadata & Risk Section
- ✅ **Metadata Table**: Better spacing (8→10pt padding), professional colors (white/gray alternating), enhanced borders
- ✅ **Risk Gauge**: Larger (3.8cm→4.2cm), bigger risk number (36→40pt), more prominent display
- ✅ **Info Table**: White/gray alternating rows, professional borders, better spacing

#### Vulnerability Section  
- ✅ **Severity Summary**: Larger text (8→12pt for counts), better spacing (5→8pt padding)
- ✅ **Vulnerability Cards**: Professional light theme, colored left borders by severity, better padding (5→7pt)
- ✅ **Components Table**: Professional dark header, white/gray rows, improved spacing

### 📊 Executive PDF (scanner/export.py)

#### Summary Section
- ✅ **Metrics Table**: Centered alignment, larger header (9→10pt), professional gray borders, better spacing (5→8pt)
- ✅ **Top Vulnerabilities**: Better formatting, larger header (8→9pt), centered alignment, professional appearance

## Visual Changes

### Colors
- Old theme: Dark background (#0d1117) - hard to read on printed paper
- New theme: Professional light (#ffffff) - perfect for printing and screens
- Better contrast: #1a1a1a dark text on light background

### Typography  
- Headers: Larger and more prominent (22→26pt main title, 12→14pt sections)
- Body: More readable (8.5→9.5pt)
- Better spacing between elements: +2-4pt throughout

### Tables & Cards
- Alternating row colors: white/#f8f9fa for better scanning
- Professional borders: #c0c0c0 main, #d4d4d4 grid
- Increased padding: 3-5pt → 5-8pt for better breathing room
- Vertical alignment: Centered for cleaner appearance
